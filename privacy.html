Privacy Policy

At AmitToolsX, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Local Processing

The most important thing to know about AmitToolsX is that your files (like the images you resize) are processed entirely on your own computer using your web browser's capabilities. We do not upload your files to our servers, and we do not store them.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect personal information, such as your name, email address, or location, unless you voluntarily provide it through our contact form. We do not use cookies to track your browsing habits across other websites.

3. External Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.

4. Security

Since your data stays on your machine, it is as secure as your own device. We use HTTPS to ensure that the code we serve you is delivered securely.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us through our contact page.
