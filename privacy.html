Privacy Policy

1. Information We Do Not Collect

At AmitToolsX, we prioritize your privacy. We want to be clear: we do not collect, store, or transmit any images you upload to our Image Resizer tool. All image processing is performed locally in your web browser using JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas API.

2. Client-Side Processing

Because all operations occur within your own browser, your data never leaves your computer. This ensures that your personal photos and professional assets remain completely private and secure.

3. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to improve your user experience, such as remembering your preferred settings. These cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information.

4. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to third-party social media platforms (Instagram, WhatsApp). Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact us via our Contact page or through our social media handles.
