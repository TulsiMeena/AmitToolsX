Privacy Protocol

01. Local First Processing

The most critical aspect of AmitToolsX is that your files (like the images you resize) are processed entirely on your own computer. We utilize modern browser APIs to handle resizing, filtering, and conversion without ever transmitting your data to a remote server.

02. Zero Data Retention

Since processing happens locally, we have zero access to your files. There is no database storing your images, and no server-side cache. When you close the tab, all temporary data is instantly wiped from your device's memory.

03. User Community

AmitToolsX is built for the community led by Aman Meena. We respect the trust you place in our tools and aim to provide a safe digital environment for all our users.

04. Contact Protocol

If you have inquiries regarding this protocol, please reach out via our contact page.
