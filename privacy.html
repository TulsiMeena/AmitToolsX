Privacy Policy

1. Data Processing

At AmitToolsX, your privacy is our top priority. Unlike other platforms, we don't store your sensitive documents on any server. Every operation happens entirely within your browser's memory.

2. User Preferences & Cookies

We use your browser's Local Storage to save your preferred theme and language. We do not use any third-party tracking cookies.

3. Optimized Performance via CDNs

To ensure blazing fast load times, we utilize industry-standard libraries like Tailwind CSS and Font Awesome served via reliable CDNs.

4. Reach Out to Us

We value your feedback. If you have concerns about your privacy, please reach out to our team via social media or the contact form.
